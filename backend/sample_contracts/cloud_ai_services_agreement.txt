ARTICLE 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 "AI Platform" means Provider's proprietary cloud-based artificial intelligence software platform, including all associated services, APIs, models, databases, documentation, and updates thereto, as described in Schedule A.

1.2 "Client Data" means any documents, contracts, pleadings, correspondence, work product, or other information uploaded, submitted, or transmitted to the AI Platform by Client or on Client's behalf, including without limitation attorney-client privileged communications.

1.3 "Output" means any analysis, summary, recommendation, flag, score, report, or other result generated by the AI Platform in response to Client Data.

1.4 "Subscription Term" means the initial period specified in Schedule A, together with any renewal periods.

1.5 "Training Data" means any data, including Client Data, that Provider may use to train, fine-tune, improve, test, benchmark, or evaluate the AI Platform or any successor model, system, or product.

2.1 License Grant. Client may not sublicense, resell, or provide access to the AI Platform to any third party, including clients of Client, without Provider's prior written consent, which may be withheld in Provider's sole discretion.

2.2 Technical Support. Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to provide email-based technical support during Provider's standard business hours (9:00 AM – 5:00 PM Pacific Time, Monday through Friday, excluding Provider's observed holidays). Provider provides no guaranteed response time for support requests. Provider does not provide telephone support.

2.3 Service Level. Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain AI Platform availability. 2.4 Modifications. Provider reserves the right to modify, discontinue, or suspend any feature or functionality of the AI Platform at any time and without notice to Client. Provider shall have no liability to Client for any such modification, discontinuation, or suspension.


ARTICLE 3. TERM AND TERMINATION

3.1 Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial Subscription Term of twelve (12) months. Thereafter, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal no later than seven (7) calendar days prior to the end of the then-current Subscription Term. For the avoidance of doubt, if Client fails to provide timely notice of non-renewal, Client shall be obligated to pay fees for the full renewal term.

3.2 Termination for Convenience. Client may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to Provider. Upon any termination for convenience by Client, Client shall pay Provider: (i) all fees and charges accrued through the termination date; (ii) a termination fee equal to seventy-five percent (75%) of all fees that would have been payable for the remainder of the then-current Subscription Term; and (iii) all setup and onboarding costs incurred by Provider in connection with this Agreement, whether or not previously invoiced.

3.3 Termination for Cause. Provider may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice to Client if: (a) Client fails to pay any amount when due; (b) Client breaches any provision of this Agreement; (c) Provider determines in its sole discretion that Client's use of the AI Platform poses a reputational, legal, or competitive risk to Provider; or (d) Provider decides to discontinue the AI Platform for any business reason.

3.4 Effect of Termination. Upon termination, all rights and licenses granted to Client shall immediately cease. Provider's obligation to delete Client Data shall be satisfied by Provider's standard data retention and deletion schedule, which Provider may modify at any time. Provider makes no commitment to return Client Data in any specific format.


ARTICLE 4. FEES AND PAYMENT

4.1 Fees. Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in Schedule A. All fees are non-refundable under all circumstances, including in the event of service outages, feature discontinuation, or termination.

4.2 Fee Increases. Provider may increase fees at any time upon thirty (30) days' notice to Client. Client's continued use of the AI Platform following notice of any fee increase shall constitute Client's acceptance of such increased fees.

4.3 Late Payments. Amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest at the rate of two percent (2%) per month, compounded monthly, from the due date until paid in full. In addition, Provider may immediately suspend Client's access to the AI Platform for any payment more than three (3) days late.

4.4 Disputed Invoices. Client must provide written notice of any invoice dispute within five (5) business days of receipt. Failure to dispute within this period constitutes irrevocable acceptance. During any dispute, Client must continue to pay the undisputed portion of any invoice.
